November Exhibiton, 'The Caption'

Posted on October 30, 2015

To all dear friends and supporters of the Wild Yak Studio, (formally known as the Hope Gallery)

We are coming up with an exhibition called ‘The Caption’ after a month of collecting captions for the photo. Now these top captions will be on display at our gallery in McLeod Ganj for the whole month of November 2015.

Thank you.

Jogiwara Road
